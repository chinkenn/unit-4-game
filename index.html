Crystal Collector!

A random number is generated at the start of the game.

Each of the crystals below is assigned a random value. Click on the crystals to add to your total.

You win if your number matches the random number exactly. You lose if your number is greater than the random number.

When you win or lose, the game will automatically reload and new values will be assigned to the crystals.
